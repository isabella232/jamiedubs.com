Jamie "Dubs" Wilkinson

I am an American computer programmer.

I was the co-founder and CEO of VHX. In 2017 we were acquired by Vimeo.

Previously, I helped create Know Your Meme, acquired by the Cheezburger Network in 2011. Some of the videos are embedded below.

In 2010 I won a Primetime Emmy for my work on Star Wars Uncut.

I was a founding member of the Free Art & Technology Lab (F.A.T.) (2007-2016) and contributed to many small and large projects, including Graffiti Markup Language, FuckFlickr, Kanye Week, Occupy Internet, and more.

I taught the Internet Famous Class at Parsons New School in 2007-2008, where students grades depended on generating Internet traffic.

There's more info about me on Wikipedia.

Contact me: @jamiew on Twitter or email "jamie@thisdomain.com"

I love open-source software and publish code on github.com/jamiew
