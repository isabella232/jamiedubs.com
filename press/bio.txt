Jamie Wilkinson is the co-founder and CEO of VHX, an eCommerce platform for film, television and video in general. He is the co-creator of Know Your Meme, taught the Internet Famous Class, won a Primetime Emmy for his work on Star Wars Uncut, and regularly speaks on the subjects of Internet culture and creative production. His work has been featured on NBC, TIME, CNN, NPR, the New York Times and the frontpage of YouTube.
